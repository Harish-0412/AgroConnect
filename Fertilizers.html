Fertilizer Usage Guide

Understand how, when, and what fertilizers to apply for healthier crops and higher yields.

Types of Fertilizers

  • Nitrogen (N): Promotes leaf growth.
  • Phosphorus (P): Supports root & flower development.
  • Potassium (K): Strengthens overall plant health.

Application Tips

Apply fertilizers based on soil test reports. Avoid over-fertilization to protect soil and environment.

Organic Options

Use compost, cow dung, neem cake, and green manure for eco-friendly farming.
